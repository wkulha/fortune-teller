Fortune Teller's Room

Tell me which of these inauspicious events has happened to you in the past two months:

A black cat crossed my path.
I walked under a ladder.
I split a salt shaker.
I saw my own doppelganger.
I broke a mirror.
I didn't respond to a Facebook chain message.
I put my shoes on the table.
I opened an umbrella inside.
A bird flew into my window.
I tripped over something.

Now tell me which of these auspicious events you've experienced in the past two months:

Bats have nested in my house.
I've seen a rainbow.
I've seen a cricket.
I found a four-leaf clover.
I found a heads-up coin on the ground and picked it up.
I've kept seeing the number 8.
My ears have been itchy.
A bird has pooped on my head.
My left hand has gone pins and needles.
I've realized I put an article of clothing on backwards and left it that way.
Good Fortune

Good fortune is coming your way. After a spell of bad luck, the cosmos has readjusted its energies in your favor.

Don't be afraid to try new things--in fact, embrace any new experiences that come your way. They will be the root of your new happiness.

The person you've been admiring from afar has noticed. Take a chance on them while your lucks is good!

Perfect Fortune

You have had more than your fair share of bad luck in recent times, but good news is around the corner.

Any business or creative ventures that you've been considering should be started without delay. The means and will come easier than you expect.

Make sure to call your parents. Their love will help propel your good luck and positive energy even farther.

Buy no less than three lottery tickets. Such good luck does not come around often, and shouldn't be wasted.

Beware!

You've been given an easy spell by the cosmos, but that luck is changing.

Shore yourself against quick losses, and take no assests or relationships for granted.

Avoid starting new ventures or relationships until your luck has changed. You will know it's changed only in the shower, when a shiver goes down your spine.
